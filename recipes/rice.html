Description

Jollof rice is a vibrant and flavorful West African dish made primarily from long-grain parboiled rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices. It is often cooked in a single pot, allowing the ingredients to meld together, creating a rich and deeply spiced base that infuses the rice with a reddish-orange hue.
